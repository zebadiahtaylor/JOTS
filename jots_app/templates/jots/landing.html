Welcome to jots

What is it?

  • A webapp for creating and sharing notes.

What's special about it?

  • Makes writing high quality notes fast and easy.
  • Enables group collaboration.
  • Auto-organizes notes with tags.
  • Simplifies gathering notes into long-form articles.
  • Easy to use.
  • Free for small groups and individual users.
  • Available in darkmode.
  • Your data will not be exploited.
  • Always getting better.

What can you use it for?

  • Take fast notes for a class or a meeting.
  • Brainstorm ideas for a project.
  • Manage the lore for your role-playing sessions
  • Record health-related information (notes are dated)
  • Use it as a journal

To get started, register or login.
